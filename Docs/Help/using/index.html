Communicating with People

Omea can serve as a central communications center where you can read incoming communications and organize them. In most cases* you can reply to incoming messages and originate new messages. This section of Help covers the communication tools available in Omea Reader:Omea:

  • E-mail
  • Instant Messaging
  • Newsgroups
  • Feeds & Weblogs
  • Contacts

 

* At this time Omea does not support direct composing and sending of instant messages from within the Omea environment.

 

Omea Reader only supports direct composing and sending of newsgroup articles from within the Omea Reader environment. Omea Pro provides support instant messaging with ICQ and Miranda, and e-mail support tightly integrated with Microsoft Outlook™ For more information, please visit www.jetbrains.com/omea.
